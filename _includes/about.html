What do we do?

Our actvites range far and wide.

  • Flying

    Loop the loops and barrel rolls

    We offer plenty of opportunities for cadets to take to the sky from experience flights to flying solo and pilots’ license

  • Field Craft

    Outdoors in the grass

    We’re not just about flying, we’re happy to get out in the fields and learn about camouflage and concealment.

  • Map Reading

    Learning about bearings

    We pride our selves on teaching skills that can be used outside of cadets as well, learning to read a map and how to follow a route are important skills.

  • Drill

    Teaching discipline

    Drill is all about timing and coordination and helps cadets work in a team. It’s also useful if any cadets choose to join the forces.
