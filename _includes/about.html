What do we do?

Our actvites range far and wide; Look below for a taste of what we do.

  • Flying

    Mastering the skies

    We offer plenty of opportunities for cadets to take to the sky from experience flights to flying solo and working towards a pilots’ license

  • Field Craft

    Surviving in the outdoors

    We’re not just about flying, we’re happy to get out and learn about camouflage, concealment, cooking and more.

  • Map Reading

    Finding your way in the world

    We pride our selves on teaching skills that can be used outside of cadets as well, learning to read a map and how to follow a route are important skills.

  • Drill

    Teaching discipline and teamwork

    Drill is all about timing, coordination and working together in a team. It’s also useful for any cadets wanting to join the forces.
