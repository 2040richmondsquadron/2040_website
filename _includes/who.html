Who Are We?

We're a military youth organisation and a part of the RAF.

Aviation

Being part of the RAF means that we can offer unique experiences in the world of aviation, not many people can say that they've flown a plane at the age of 13.

Training

We offer a comprehensive training system that teaches you skills that can applied both in and outside of the RAF, for example we offer several BTEC's.

Adventure

We're not always in the air, when we're on the ground we do a range of things from the Duke of Edinburgh to Sport to First Aid to Field Craft.
